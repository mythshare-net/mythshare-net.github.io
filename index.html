The Lore

The Mabinogion

The Mabinogion is the earliest prose stories of the literature of Britain. These tales include the Four Branches of the Mabinogi, native Welsh tales, and Arthurian romances.

Annwn - The Otherworld

In Welsh mythology, Annwn is the Otherworld, ruled by Arawn or Gwyn ap Nudd. It is a realm of eternal youth and abundance, home to the C≈µn Annwn (Hounds of Annwn).

The Tylwyth Teg

The Tylwyth Teg, or "Fair Family", are the Welsh fairies. These beings inhabit lakes, rivers, and hills, and are known for their enchanting music and magical abilities.
